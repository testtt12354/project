Indian Butter Chicken

Homemade Shoyu Ramen

  • 16 dried shiitake mushrooms

  • 20 g dried bonito flakes (about 2 c. loosely packed)
  • 4 cold large eggs
  • 2 1/2 c. reserved chashu-tare liquid

California Roll

Pad Thai

  • 3 Tbsp. palm sugar
  • 3 Tbsp. Thai fish sauce
